# Pilot Checklist

How to run a Sentinel pilot, start to finish.

## Before the pilot (founder prep)

- [ ] Confirm the prospect matches the ICP (`docs/product/ideal-customer-profile.md`):
      20-200 person B2B SaaS, 5-20 support agents, already on Intercom/Zendesk/Freshdesk.
- [ ] Confirm you know their buying trigger -- a specific recent moment of
      frustration, not a general "we should look at tools" feeling.
- [ ] Have `docs/customer/onboarding-call.md` open during the first call.

## Day 0 -- onboarding call

- [ ] Walk through signup live, or have them do it while you watch.
- [ ] Confirm they connect a real source before the call ends -- don't let
      them leave with an empty workspace. An empty workspace by day 3 is
      the single biggest predictor of a pilot that never activates.
- [ ] Point at the first insight together. This is the moment that sells
      the rest of the pilot -- don't rush past it.
- [ ] Tell them explicitly: "click the feedback button any time something
      doesn't make sense -- I read every one."

## Week 1

- [ ] Check the founder dashboard (`/dashboard/founder`) for: did they
      connect a source? Did they establish a baseline? Any feedback yet?
- [ ] If no activity by day 3, reach out proactively -- don't wait for them
      to come back on their own.

## Day 7

- [ ] Send a short check-in (see `docs/customer/follow-up-email.md`).
- [ ] Ask: did they act on anything Sentinel found? This is the real
      activation signal, not login count.

## Day 30 -- evaluate

- [ ] Review `docs/product/activation-funnel.md`'s definition of activated.
- [ ] Run the interview from `docs/customer/interview-template.md`.
- [ ] Decide, based on the interview, not on a hunch: would this customer
      say Sentinel is worth paying for in their own words?

## What success looks like after 30 days

See `docs/customer/success-metrics.md` for the specific bar. In short: at
least one real change was made because of something Sentinel found, and the
customer would keep the product if asked to choose today.
