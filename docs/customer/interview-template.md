# Customer Interview Template

Fill one of these out per pilot interview. The point of a template is
consistency across interviews -- so that after five pilots, answers can be
compared side by side instead of living in five different sets of loose
notes. Use alongside the question-asking guidance in
`docs/product/customer-interview-guide.md` (that doc explains *how* to ask
without leading; this one is the record of what was said).
