# Getting Started Guide (Phase 18F)

For the first customer-success conversation with a pilot customer -- what to
walk them through, in order.

## 1. Connect your support system

From the dashboard, click **Connect SupportOS**. This is the only step --
there's no separate "authorize," "configure," or "map fields" screen.
Clicking the button reads your existing tickets and conversations and turns
them into signals Sentinel can analyze.

If you don't see a "Connect" button and instead see your full dashboard
already, your workspace has already connected a source -- look for
**Sync Now** in the Connected Sources card instead.

## 2. Run your first analysis

Connecting *is* running the first analysis -- there's no separate step.
Within moments of connecting, you'll see **"Your first Sentinel report is
ready"** with the number of conversations and tickets analyzed, and the
first pattern Sentinel found.

## 3. Review patterns

Look at the **"Biggest opportunity"** on your first insight card, and the
**Emerging Risks** section once you have a full dashboard -- these are the
patterns Sentinel found by grouping similar conversations together, not a
single alert. Click into a finding to see the evidence behind it (which
conversations it came from, how often it's happening).

Click **"Establish Baseline"** once you're ready to have Sentinel track
whether things are improving from here -- this turns your first insight
into your first official health report, the number every future report gets
compared against.

## 4. Improve operations

Pick one finding, make the change it suggests (usually: fix a documentation
gap, update a macro, clarify a confusing step), and mark the finding
resolved. The next time Sentinel sees that pattern recur -- or not recur --
it becomes part of your **Operational Memory**: a record of what you tried
and whether it worked, that Sentinel surfaces the next time something
similar comes up.

That loop -- notice, fix, measure, remember -- is the whole product. Everything
else in the dashboard (health score, trends, executive brief) is a different
view onto the same underlying loop.

## If you get stuck

See the public FAQ for specific failure-mode answers ("what happens if sync
fails," "what happens if AI is unavailable"), or use the **Feedback** button
in the bottom-right corner of any dashboard page -- a real person reads
every message.
